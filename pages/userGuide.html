Registration

To be able to evaluate or connect to our API Products in any environment, you must register on the Developer Portal.

Important Information
  • After submission, you'll receive activation instructions via email
  • Account activation is required to:
    • View technical specifications
    • Evaluate/test L+T API products
    • Create system connections

Sign In

After registration, sign in to access various features and functions that enable you to evaluate and consume Equifax API products.

Change / Reset Account Password

For security reasons, you will periodically be forced to change your password.

Important Information
  • You can change or reset your password at any time
  • Your new password cannot be one previously used
  • Password requirements are shown during the reset process

My Account & Company Profile

Anytime after you have registered (and are signed-in, you can update your Account and your Company Information.

Important Information
  • You can change your account password.
  • You cannot change your account email address.
  • In  your My Company Profile, it is important that you provide complete and accurate information. The more company profile fields you populate In addition to the required fields, the better Equifax is able to serve your needs when promoting your apps to higher environments.

Where do I find it?

Hover on your first name in the top-right corner of the page, and select My Account or My Company as appropriate.

Transferring Your Account

You cannot transfer your account to another user, however, you can transfer apps you own to other users. See Application Owner Change to learn more.

Remove / Disable Account

You cannot remove your account. If you want to have your account disabled, use the CONTACT US link in the top navigation.
